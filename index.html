About Me

Back Story

In September 2018 I started to teach myself the basics of HTML5 and CSS using online resources. I started out using online coding tutorials from codecadmy.com and codebar.io. I also started attending weekly workshops hosted by codebar.io in various London locations. In March 2019 I signed up for a 4 week introduction course in Web Development hosted by Founders and Coders. All of the resources that I have had access to, have been extremely helpful and have encouraged me to continue towards a career in Web Development. I am also learning Javascript through the tutorials on codecademy.com, codebar.io and freecodecamp.org.
